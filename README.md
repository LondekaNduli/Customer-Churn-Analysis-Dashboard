# Customer-Churn-Analysis-Dashboard
Power BI dashboard analyzing customer churn patterns, contract behavior, and payment methods. Utilized Power Query for data cleaning and DAX for KPI calculations to identify key drivers of customer attrition.

# Key Insights

### Customer Churn Overview

* The company has approximately 1,200 customers, of which 432 have churned.
* The overall churn rate is 36%, indicating that more than one-third of customers have discontinued the service.
* The average monthly charge across customers is 69.53.

### Contract Type Analysis

* Customers on month-to-month contracts exhibit the highest churn levels compared to customers on one-year and two-year contracts.
* Long-term contracts are associated with significantly lower churn rates, suggesting that contract commitment improves customer retention.

### Monthly Charges and Churn

* Customers who churn tend to have monthly charges comparable to or slightly higher than retained customers.
* Pricing and perceived value may influence customer retention and should be investigated further.

### Payment Method Analysis

* Customers using electronic check payments show higher churn volumes compared to other payment methods.
* Customers using automatic payment methods appear more likely to remain with the company.

### Business Implications

* Month-to-month customers represent the highest retention risk segment.
* Encouraging customers to move to longer-term contracts may reduce churn.
* Investigating customer experiences associated with electronic check payments could help improve retention.
* Targeted retention campaigns should focus on customers with short-term contracts and higher monthly charges.

## Conclusion

This dashboard identifies key drivers of customer churn through contract type, payment method, and billing analysis. The findings suggest that customers on month-to-month contracts are significantly more likely to churn than those on longer-term agreements. By promoting long-term contracts, improving customer engagement, and targeting high-risk customer segments, the business can reduce churn and improve customer lifetime value.
